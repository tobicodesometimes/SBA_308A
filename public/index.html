Naruto Character Explorer

Synopsis: Twelve years before the start of the series, the ninja village of Konohagakure was attacked by the Nine-Tailed Demon Fox. The village was only saved from complete destruction by the actions of Konoha's leader, the Fourth Hokage, who sacrificed his life to seal the Nine-Tails into a newborn: Naruto Uzumaki. Orphaned by the attack, Naruto grew up shunned by most of the villagers, who hated and feared him because of the Nine-Tails trapped within him. Unaware of the monster he contained, Naruto longed for acknowledgment, and so vowed to one day become the greatest Hokage the village had ever seen.

Naruto, created by Masashi Kishimoto, follows Naruto Uzumaki — an orphaned ninja who dreams of becoming Hokage (Head of the Village) —in a world of hidden villages, chakra, and bloodline techniques; the manga ran from 1999 ~ 2014 and the anime aired from 2002 ~ 2017 (Naruto + Shippūden); its sequel Boruto: Naruto Next Generations continues with the next generation and branches into its own storyline.
